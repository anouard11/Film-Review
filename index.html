What is a Film Review?

A complete review must include all of these key elements

Title of the Film
Director
Country
Length / Duration
Year of Release
Producer
Main Actors
Story Summary
Main Characters
Special Effects
Opinion
Recommendation

Key Definitions

Learn these words — you will need them to write your review

1
Director
The person who controls how the movie is made.
2
Producer
The person who organizes and pays for the movie.
3
Special Effects
Visual effects that make the movie look exciting or realistic.
4
Performance
How well the actors act in the movie.
5
Message
The lesson or idea the movie teaches.

Film Review Template

Three structured paragraphs — study the template, then read the examples

1 — Introduction
Title · Director · Country · Type of Film · Length · Year · Producer
The film [Title] is directed by [Director]. It was produced in [Country]. It is a [type of film] with a length of [length/time]. The film was released in [year] and produced by [Producer].
🏠 Alone at Home

The film Alone at Home is directed by Chris Columbus. It was produced in USA. It is a family comedy with a length of 103 minutes. The film was released in 1990 and produced by John Hughes.

🐼 Kung Fu Panda

The film Kung Fu Panda is directed by Mark Osborne and John Stevenson. It was produced in USA. It is an animated action-comedy with a length of 92 minutes. The film was released in 2008 and produced by Melissa Cobb.

🥋 The Karate Kid (Jackie Chan version)

The film The Karate Kid is directed by Harald Zwart. It was produced in USA/China. It is an action-drama with a length of 140 minutes. The film was released in 2010 and produced by Jerry Weintraub.

2 — Body
Story · Main Actors · Main Characters · Performance · Special Effects · Message
The story is about [brief summary]. The main actors are [actors]. The main characters are [characters]. The performances were [comment on acting], and the special effects were [comment on visuals/effects]. The message of the film is [lesson/moral/theme].
🏠 Alone at Home

The story is about a young boy left home alone who has to defend his house from burglars. The main actors are Macaulay Culkin, Joe Pesci, and Daniel Stern. The main characters are Kevin McCallister and the burglars Harry and Marv. The performances were funny and convincing, and the special effects were minimal but effective for comedy. The message of the film is the importance of family and clever thinking.

🐼 Kung Fu Panda

The story is about a clumsy panda named Po who dreams of becoming a kung fu master. The main actors are Jack Black, Dustin Hoffman, and Angelina Jolie. The main characters are Po, Master Shifu, and Tai Lung. The performances were expressive and entertaining, and the special effects were amazing animation and fight sequences. The message of the film is believe in yourself and follow your destiny.

🥋 The Karate Kid (Jackie Chan version)

The story is about a boy who moves to a new country and learns martial arts to defend himself and gain confidence. The main actors are Jaden Smith, Jackie Chan, and Taraji P. Henson. The main characters are Dre Parker and Mr. Han. The performances were emotional and inspiring, and the special effects were realistic martial arts choreography. The message of the film is discipline, respect, and perseverance.

3 — Conclusion
Opinion · Recommendation
I liked / didn't like the film because [reason]. I would recommend / not recommend it to others because [reason].
🏠 Alone at Home

I liked the film because it is funny and entertaining for all ages. I would recommend it to others because it is a classic family comedy.

🐼 Kung Fu Panda

I liked the film because it is exciting, humorous, and beautifully animated. I would recommend it to others because it teaches important lessons while being fun to watch.

🥋 The Karate Kid (Jackie Chan version)

I liked the film because it has inspiring performances and exciting action scenes. I would recommend it to others because it shows the importance of discipline and courage.

Model Review

Study this complete example — copy and adapt the structure for your own review

★ Model Answer
"12 Angry Men"
1957 · Sidney Lumet · Drama · 96 min

Introduction "12 Angry Men" is one of the best films I have ever watched. It is an American drama and courtroom film. It was made in 1957 and directed by Sidney Lumet. The film lasts for 96 minutes.

Body The story of the film is about twelve men who have to decide if a boy is guilty of killing his father. One juror tries to make the others think carefully about the evidence. As they talk, the men start to share their opinions and feelings. The actors Henry Fonda and Lee J. Cobb acted very well in this movie. The film has no special effects, but the story and acting make it feel very real.

Conclusion I really enjoyed this film and I think that people should see it because the story is interesting and it teaches a good lesson about fairness.
